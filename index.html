The number of teams will determine the number of scrimmages. Each team will have at least 2 scrimmages.

Rules
• Each team will have 15 minutes Offense. 15 minutes Defense or go through kicking line up and switch. Coaches’ choice.
• Team on Defense coach will be referee or can have a parent.
• Teams are encouraged to help each other and Teach CYO Rules.
• Dress Tournament tee shirt or team uniform.
• We ask that teams leave after their session is over so other teams can enter.

** If your team orders tee shirts, pick up the day of scrimmage at Tee Shirt Table.
